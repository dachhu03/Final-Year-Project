First Aid Tips

Burns

If you or someone else gets a burn, here are the steps to take:

  • Cool the burn with cool (not cold) water for at least 10 minutes.
  • Cover the burn with a sterile, non-adhesive bandage or clean cloth.
  • Avoid using ice, butter, or ointments on the burn.
  • Seek medical attention if the burn is severe.

Cuts and Scrapes

For minor cuts and scrapes, follow these steps:

  • Clean the wound with water and mild soap.
  • Apply an antibiotic ointment to prevent infection.
  • Cover the wound with a sterile bandage or gauze.
  • Change the dressing regularly to keep the wound clean.
